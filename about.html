About Us

In My Way is an organisation committed to education and advocacy for accessibility and inclusion in the Naarm live music scene.

Disability led and not for profit, In My Way seeks to challenges assumptions about the ways that people can and do enjoy live music, and offer free resources and information on the ways that live music venues and promoters can extend access to their events, in order to allow a truly diverse community of music loves and makers to thrive.

To stay up to date with all the latest news in live music Accessibility and Inclusivity, and for spotlights on upcoming inclusive local events, follow In My Way on Instagram!

In My Way acknowledges and pays respect to the Traditional Custodians and Elders of the land on which we work, the Wurundjeri Woi-wurrung people, and acknowledge that sovereignty was never ceded.
